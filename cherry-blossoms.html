CHERRY BLOSSOMS

Nature's Poetry in Bloom

Experience the Magic of Sakura

Cherry blossoms, or sakura, are Japan's most famous natural phenomenon. These delicate pink flowers symbolize the beauty and transience of life, creating a breathtaking spectacle that attracts visitors from around the world.

Best Viewing Spots

  • • Shinjuku Gyoen, Tokyo
  • • Philosopher's Path, Kyoto
  • • Hirosaki Castle, Aomori
  • • Mount Yoshino, Nara
  • • Ueno Park, Tokyo

Viewing Season

  • • Late March to early April
  • • Peak bloom lasts about 1 week
  • • Varies by region and weather
  • • Check forecast for exact dates

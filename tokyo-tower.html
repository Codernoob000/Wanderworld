TOKYO TOWER

The Icon of Japan's Skyline

Experience Tokyo from Above

Standing at 333 meters tall, Tokyo Tower is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Japan. This iconic structure offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and serves as a symbol of Japan's post-war rebirth.

Highlights

  • • Main Deck at 150 meters
  • • Top Deck at 250 meters
  • • Night illumination shows
  • • Foot Town shopping area
  • • One Piece Tower theme park

Visitor Information

  • • Open daily: 9:00 AM - 11:00 PM
  • • Best time: Sunset for city views
  • • Location: Minato City, Tokyo
  • • Access: Multiple subway lines

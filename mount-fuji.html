MOUNT FUJI

Japan's Majestic Icon

Climb Japan's Highest Peak

Standing at 3,776 meters, Mount Fuji is Japan's highest mountain and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This iconic volcano offers breathtaking views and a challenging yet rewarding climbing experience.

Climbing Information

  • • Official climbing season: July to September
  • • Four main trails to the summit
  • • 5-7 hours to reach the top
  • • Mountain huts available
  • • Sunrise views from the summit

Viewing Spots

  • • Lake Kawaguchiko
  • • Chureito Pagoda
  • • Fuji Five Lakes
  • • Hakone Ropeway
